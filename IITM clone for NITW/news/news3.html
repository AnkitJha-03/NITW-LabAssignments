Ek Pad Maa Ke Naam Campaign

1 Aug 2024 | NITW Press

NIT Warangal's "Ek Pad Maa Ke Naam" campaign focused on environmental conservation, encouraging students and staff to plant a tree in honor of their mothers. The initiative aimed to raise awareness about sustainability and strengthen the bond with nature.



The campaign began with a special event where students, faculty, and staff were invited to plant trees in designated areas on campus. The initiative was driven by the desire to create a green and sustainable environment while promoting environmental responsibility among the younger generation.

Environmental Impact

The campaign emphasized the importance of sustainability and environmental conservation. Each tree planted symbolized a commitment to a greener future, fostering a connection between individuals and the natural world.


Strengthening the Bond with Nature

The symbolic gesture of planting a tree in honor of one's mother was meant to inspire participants to take greater responsibility for the planet. The campaign helped raise awareness about the environment and inspired many to adopt eco-friendly practices.
