| Details

NIT Warangal will host a special event on Gandhi Jayanti, celebrating Mahatma Gandhi's life and teachings. The program will start with a speech by the Director, followed by cultural performances that showcase Gandhi's impact on India's freedom struggle.

A tree plantation drive will also take place, encouraging students and faculty to contribute to environmental conservation, reflecting Gandhi's values of harmony with nature. The event will feature an exhibition of photographs and artifacts related to Gandhi, providing insights into his life and principles.

Participants will conclude the day with a pledge-taking ceremony, committing to uphold Gandhi's ideals of non-violence and truth in their daily lives. This event aims to inspire the NIT Warangal community to live by the principles that Gandhi stood for, promoting peace and sustainability.

| Campus Life

The NIT Warangal campus is spread over 256 acres of lush greenery, providing a serene environment for academic and extracurricular activities. It boasts modern infrastructure, including well-equipped laboratories, a vast library, and state-of-the-art lecture halls. The campus also features numerous hostels, sports facilities, and recreational areas that cater to the diverse needs of its students, fostering both personal and academic growth. With a vibrant atmosphere and strong focus on research and innovation, NIT Warangal's campus is an ideal place for holistic development.
