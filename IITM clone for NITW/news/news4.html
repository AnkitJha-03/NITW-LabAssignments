Organizing a Medical Camp in Campus

8 July 2024 | NITW Press

NIT Warangal organized a medical camp on campus, providing free health check-ups and consultations for students, faculty, and staff. The camp aimed to promote health awareness and offer medical support to the campus community.



The camp included various health check-ups such as blood pressure monitoring, sugar level tests, eye examinations, and general health consultations. Doctors and medical staff from renowned hospitals were invited to conduct these sessions.

Health Check-ups and Consultations

The camp offered a range of free health check-ups, including screenings for diabetes, blood pressure, and vision. Participants also had the opportunity to consult with doctors about general health issues and receive advice on maintaining a healthy lifestyle.


Promoting Health Awareness

The event highlighted the importance of regular health check-ups and preventive care. It also provided a platform for students and staff to address any health concerns with medical professionals.
