Celebration of National Sports Day (26th to 31st August 2024)

28 Sep 2024 | NITW Press

NIT Warangal celebrated National Sports Day from August 26th to 31st, 2024, with sports events, competitions, and fitness workshops, promoting teamwork and recognizing outstanding athletes.



NIT Warangal proudly celebrated National Sports Day from August 26th to 31st, 2024, embracing the spirit of sportsmanship and fitness. The week kicked off with an opening ceremony featuring a flag hoisting, where the Director and esteemed faculty members emphasized the importance of sports in education and personal development. Throughout the week, various sports events were organized, including football, basketball, cricket, and badminton, drawing significant participation from students.

Sports Events and Workshops

The celebration included multiple sports competitions and fitness workshops to engage students and faculty members. The football and basketball tournaments were the highlights of the week, with teams from different departments fiercely competing for victory. Badminton matches showcased individual and doubles talent, bringing excitement to the spectators.

In addition to these events, fitness workshops such as yoga, aerobics, and wellness talks were held to promote physical and mental well-being. Experts from the health and fitness industry were invited to share their insights on maintaining a balanced lifestyle, drawing large crowds for these sessions.


Awards and Recognition

The closing ceremony was a grand affair where outstanding athletes were recognized and honored with awards. Students who excelled in various sports were awarded trophies, certificates, and special recognition from the Director. The event closed with a renewed commitment to fostering a culture of physical fitness and wellness on campus.
